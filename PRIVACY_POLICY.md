# Privacy Policy

AquaMind does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app uses Apple system services such as notifications and in-app purchases.
All payment processes are handled securely by Apple through the App Store.
AquaMind does not have access to your payment information.

Any health or hydration data entered in the app is stored locally on the user's device
and is not transmitted to any external servers.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer via GitHub.
